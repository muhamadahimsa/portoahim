BaC

BaC, where timeless charm meets the warmth of a neighborhood bakery and the comfort of a traditional coffeehouse. at BaC, we believe in the beauty of simplicity, and BaC is more than just a bakery, it's place where people gather to savor life's simple pleasures. When designing BaC I was referring to its characteristics, namely wanting to give the feel and feeling of a childhood home.
